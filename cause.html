my cause

Materially, there has never been a more bountiful time in the western world. Devices that can tap into the largest ever assembled font of human knowledge have proliferated at scale. Literacy is the highest it's ever been, lack of plumbing or heating is a rarity. Food is being produced in greater and greater quantities, such that as soon as a break in the 'just on time' delivery chain occurs, tonnes of that produce will simply be trashed. We've attained a breadth and depth of material wealth vast enough that people can afford to say things like this.
