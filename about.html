professionally

I'm a fledgling developer, and I'm expanding my knowledge base by the day. My favorite language so far is C. But I also know Java and Javascript. At the moment, I have the most experience and proven work in web development, using the HTML/CSS/Javascript stack along with Bootstrap.

As I improve myself, I'll come back to this site and expand upon it. For now I've taken a brutalist approach to the visuals, and eschewed clutter to make for a straightforward browsing experience. I also focused on making sure this site was simple to scale and add on to, as my experience grows and I have more projects to list.

personally

I'm a voracious reader. I prefer C.S. Lewis over Tolkien, and Gene Wolfe over Lovecraft. But if you asked me to pick between the four of them, I'd be completely stumped. Besides fiction, I also read philosophy, mythology, ancient history and the Bible.

My taste in music mainly consists of jazz, but I branch out into too many other genres to name. Right now I like Japanese PC-98 game OSTs. My current personal project is a TF2 web-app using the bp.tf API and the Steam API to calculate loadout prices based on current market values.
